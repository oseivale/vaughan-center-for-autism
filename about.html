Our Story

We believe that every child can achieve academic success when given the opportunity. No child should be denied academic achievement because of developmental differences.

Vaughan Centre for Autism provides special-needs tutoring for individuals living with autism and other developmental disorders. Our special education intervention program is a personalized program designed to accommodate and elevate the studentâ€™s current academic development to the level required by the Ontario School Curriculum.

We will not relent in our effort to bridge the gap that exists between the student and the curriculum. We will provide excellent tutoring to help each student achieve his or her optimum academic potential.

As a result, we create an environment that allows us to learn from the individuals we work with in such a way as to make a meaningful contribution to their development.
