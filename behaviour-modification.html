Behaviour Modification Program

We work collaboratively with parents and other caregivers to successfully prevent the reoccurrence of a problem behavior.

Our behavior modification program aims at providing the opportunity for discovery, learning and providing a creative solution for treating maladaptive behaviors. We combine therapy and education in our approach to eliminate problem behaviors.

We have an effective autism treatment program intended to provide a comprehensive care for challenging behaviors.
