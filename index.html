We Aim For Success

Our programs recognize the diversity that exists among students with special needs. We believe that a student with special needs can learn successfully when given the opportunity.

Our Belief

We believe that individuals living with autism and other developmental challenges hold the key to overcoming their individual challenges. As a result, we create an environment that allows us to learn from the individuals we work with in such a way as to make a meaningful contribution to their development.

Our Mission

To empower individuals living with autism and other developmental disorders to reach their optimal potential in education and community living

Our Programs

Speech & Language Development

We also make house calls for students who prefer private tutoring at their home. We have experienced tutors who know how to help students to be organized and become self- starters. Our in-home, customized tutoring is innovative with proven and guaranteed results. Learn More

Behaviour Modification Program

Our behaviour modification program aims at providing the opportunity discover, learning and providing a creative solution for treating maladaptive behaviour. We combine therapy and education in our approach to eliminate problem behaviour. Learn More

Academic or Educational Problems

We treat clinically diagnosed academic or educational problems such as low-level literacy, underachievement and task avoidance. Learn More

Occupational Challenges

We are able to treat clinically diagnosed impairements such as toileting incontinence, writing challenges , employment and other psychological stressors. Learn More

Our Values

We are a community of parents of individuals with autism and other developmental challenges who have come together to help individuals with special needs achieve academic success.

  • Making a difference
  • Upholding transparency
  • Staying committed to honest relationships

We believe that every child can achieve academic success when given the opportunity. No child should be denied academic achievement because of developmental differences.
